# Practice – Service Analysis

## Purpose

Train service-level attack thinking.

---

## Process

1. identify service
2. map inputs
3. map outputs
4. identify trust boundaries

---

## Questions

- what does it accept?
- what does it return?
- what does it trust?

---

## Examples

### Web

- parameters
- headers
- uploads

### SMB

- shares
- permissions

### SSH

- auth methods
- key usage

---

## Goal

Understand:
→ how the service can be abused

---

## Golden Rule

Every service exposes an attack surface
