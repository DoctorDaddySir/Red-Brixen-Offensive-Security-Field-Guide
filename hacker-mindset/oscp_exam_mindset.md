# OSCP Exam Mindset

## Purpose

Optimize behavior during the exam.

---

## Core Principles

- stay calm
- stay systematic
- avoid rabbit holes

---

## Time Strategy

0–30 min:
- full enumeration
- quick wins

30–90 min:
- targeted attacks

90+:
- pivot or switch target

---

## When Stuck

- switch technique
- switch target
- revisit enumeration

---

## Common Failures

- tunnel vision
- overcomplication
- skipping basics

---

## Priority Order

1. credentials
2. misconfigurations
3. simple exploits
4. complex exploits (last)

---

## Golden Rules

- enumeration solves most problems
- credentials win exams
- simple beats clever
