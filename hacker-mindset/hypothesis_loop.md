# Hypothesis Loop

## Purpose

Create a repeatable process for solving unknown problems.

---

## The Loop

1. Observe
2. Hypothesize
3. Test
4. Evaluate
5. Repeat

---

## Example

Observation:
SMB share exists

Hypothesis:
Contains credentials

Test:
Download files

Evaluate:
No creds → new hypothesis

---

## Rules

- keep hypotheses small
- test quickly
- discard failed ideas immediately

---

## When Stuck

- generate 3 new hypotheses
- test all within 10 minutes

---

## Anti-Pattern

- overthinking before testing
- sticking to one idea too long

---

## Golden Rule

Speed of iteration > correctness of first idea
